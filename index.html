Key Tips
  • Octopus Card: Get at HKG arrival hall. Covers all HK MTR, buses, trams, shops — and Shenzhen Metro if you top up Mainland wallet.
  • HKG → Shenzhen: West Kowloon high-speed rail to Shenzhen North/Futian: 14–25 min, ~¥65. Every 5–15 min, no booking needed.
  • Shenzhen → Macau ferry: Shekou Ferry Terminal (Metro Line 2). ~1h, ~¥200–280. Book online during Golden Week.
  • Alipay / WeChat Pay: Link a foreign card before mainland China entry. Most vendors only accept QR payment.
  • Zhongshan Hot Springs (中山温泉): The original "Zhongshan Hot Spring Resort" opened 1980 — China's first joint-venture resort. Day admission or evening session available. Ideal for the family rest day.
  • Guilin + Zhangjiajie after Oct 5: Crowds drop 60–70%, prices fall, autumn foliage begins.
  • Forbidden City (Beijing): Book on official WeChat mini-program, up to 7 days ahead (~¥60). Sells out. Book the evening you arrive.
  • PEK Airport T3: 24h food courts, comfortable seating, Priority Pass lounges. Check-in opens ~04:00 for morning flights.
